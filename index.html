About Me

Born and raised in Fairfax Virginia, I attended university in South Korea to study business and the Korean language. Upon graduation, I worked at a few mobile gaming companies to help localize, publish, and operate some of the world's top ranking games. While working in the mobile gaming industry, I became interested in getting behind the scenes, and learning how to code myself. I started learning early 2015, but I did not take it too seriously until mid 2016. I am now looking for a chance to break into the industry of the creation of the interwebs!

I am someone who strongly believes in learning by doing. As a business degree holder, I have no formal coding education, but I continue to learn and improve every day. I am constantly on the search for new and exciting technologies that will change the world. You should never do anything half-way. If you start something, you need to finish it. The hardest part of any race is always the last mile.
